Superfancy Resort

Welcome to Superfancy Resort!!

Superfancy Resort is located in the heart of downtown Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Our expansive, 100-acre compound is only a short city bus ride away from premium strip malls, distinguished cultural touchstones, and well-known culinary landmarks. Our staff is well-paid, fully documented, and prepared to serve your every need at a moment's notice. You're going to love it here!!

Our ammenities include an onsite pool table, table tenis courts, shuffle board, an above ground pool, cinemax, and much more! We currently a dry resort, so leave your acoholic beverages at home! We can accommodate up to 250 guests in our luxurious dormitory style rooms. Complete with a shared kitchenette and communal bathrooms. We boast an expansive blacktop patio on our roof, featuring two basketball courts and a foursquare court.
